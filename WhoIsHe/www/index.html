Rick Khol of Wichita, Kansas has worn many hats through the years. He’s the father of eight boys, former missionary to South America, retired science teacher, church elder, foster parent, and Christian camp and conference speaker. Because of his interest in church history, he has a Bible museum in his basement. As Rick progressed in his study of the names of Christ, he felt drawn closer to “Him who is true” and wanted to share this experience with others. Hopefully, this devotional will help to bring others to a deeper relationship to the Lord of Glory.

- Rick Khol
